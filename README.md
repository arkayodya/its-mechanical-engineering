# ITS Mechanical Engineering Archive ⚙️

Welcome to my digital engineering notebook and portfolio. This repository serves as a permanent, structured archive of my journey through the Mechanical Engineering department at Institut Teknologi Sepuluh Nopember (ITS), spanning from my freshman year to graduation.

The goal of this repository is to bridge theoretical academic concepts with practical execution, archiving verified competencies in CAD, computation, embedded systems, and applied engineering design.

---

## 📌 Core Engineering Philosophy
* **Documentation is Design:** An engineering solution isn't complete unless it is clearly communicated, documented, and reproducible.
* **First-Principles Thinking:** Breaking complex problems down to basic physics before writing code or modeling parts.
* **Iterative Failure:** Embracing physical and digital prototypes that fail, analyzing the root cause, and documenting the redesign.
